Key Constraints

Know what's permanent before you click.

Some fields are locked forever after saving. No UI-level edits — database fix only.

🔒 Cannot change after saving

  • Company Name — typo = permanent, blocks all SKU uploads
  • Brand Name — can only add, never remove
  • Seller Lat/Long (warehouse coordinates)
  • Seller Mobile Number (used for OTP login)

✏️ Can add or extend later

  • New brands under an existing Company
  • New Companies mapped to a Seller
  • Additional brands mapped to a Seller
  • New Serviceability beats and polygons
FAQs

Common admin questions.

Quick answers before you make an irreversible change.

Can I edit a Company name after saving it?

No. Company names cannot be edited through the UI after creation. A typo is permanent unless fixed at the database level by the engineering team. Always confirm the exact legal entity name — and spelling — before clicking Create.

⚠ The Company Name is used character-by-character in every seller's SKU upload file. A single dot or space difference causes every row referencing that manufacturer to fail.
Can I remove a brand from a Company?

No. Brands can only be added, never removed. You can add new brands to a Company at any time, but existing ones cannot be deleted. Confirm the full brand list with the seller before creating the Company to avoid polluting the dropdown.

What happens if I enter the wrong Lat/Long for a seller?

The Lat/Long coordinates become the seller's warehouse and are permanently locked after saving. They determine the centre of every serviceability beat and the origin of every dispatch. The seller cannot change them from their portal.

⚠ Always capture coordinates from Google Maps at the seller's actual godown or dispatch point — not a generic city centre. Incorrect coordinates require a database-level fix.
Is Serviceability mandatory before a seller can receive orders?

Yes, absolutely. A Beat Name and Delivery Day must be configured for each Company the seller carries. Without it, orders cannot route to that seller regardless of how many SKUs they have. The GeoJSON polygon can be added later — but beat name and delivery day are required at setup.

What's the difference between "All Brands" and "Specific Brands" mapping?

"All Brands" gives the seller every current and future brand of that Company automatically — new brands added later flow in with zero action required. "Specific Brands" requires manual selection, and any brand added to the Company later will NOT appear for that seller until you manually add it. Choose based on whether the distributor carries the full company line or a subset.

Can two sellers have overlapping serviceability polygons?

Yes — overlapping polygons are permitted in Phase 1. It is an operational decision, not a system constraint. Multiple sellers can cover the same territory; the routing logic will handle which seller receives a given order.

I see a duplicate Company in the list. What should I do?

Do not map any new sellers to the duplicate. Escalate to the engineering team for a database-level cleanup. A duplicate Company name causes a permanent data fork — sellers mapped to the duplicate won't see SKUs that referenced the original, and there is no UI-level merge tool.

The seller's SKU upload keeps failing with "Manufacturer not found" even though the Company exists.

Compare the exact Company name in Super Admin with what the seller has typed in the Manufacturer column of their SKU file — character by character. Check for trailing spaces, different capitalisation, or abbreviation differences (e.g. "Pvt. Ltd." vs "PVT LTD"). The match must be exact; there is no fuzzy matching.
