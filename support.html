FAQs

Quick answers.

The most common questions from sellers and admins.

GeneralWhat's the difference between the Seller Portal and Super Admin Portal?

The Super Admin Portal is used by Qwipo's internal team to set up distributors — creating companies, brands, seller profiles, and serviceability. The Seller Portal is used by distributors to manage their catalogue, orders, pricing schemes, and store settings. Super Admin setup must be completed before a seller can use their portal.

SellerMy SKU rows are failing with "Manufacturer not found." What's wrong?

The Manufacturer field must exactly match the Company name saved by your Super Admin — character-by-character, including dots, spaces, and capitalisation. There is no fuzzy matching. Contact your Super Admin to get the exact Company name, then copy-paste it directly into the file.

SellerWhat units are accepted by ONDC?

Only four units are accepted: Gram, Kilogram, Litre, and Millilitre. If your products use Pieces, Pack, or Nos, use Gram as a temporary workaround — this ONDC limitation is under review and will be resolved in a future phase.

SellerI imported SKUs successfully but buyers still can't see them. Why?

After a successful import, all SKUs show "Non-compliant" because product images are missing. ONDC requires at least one image per SKU before it appears in any buyer app. Run the sku-image-upload automated process to upload images and flip the status to Compliant.

SellerI'm not receiving any orders. What should I check?

Confirm that your Super Admin has configured Serviceability for your account — specifically the Beat Name and Delivery Day for each Company you carry. Without Serviceability, orders cannot route to you regardless of whether your SKUs are live and compliant.

SellerCan I change my warehouse address?

No. Your warehouse address and coordinates are set when your Super Admin creates your Seller profile and are permanently locked. If the coordinates are incorrect, contact your Super Admin — it requires a database-level correction and cannot be done through the portal.

SellerThe dashboard shows "Dashboard is on the way." Is something broken?

No, this is expected in Phase 1. Dashboard analytics and sales KPIs will be available in a future release. All five sidebar modules — My SKU, Customers, Offers & Schemes, Orders, and Settings — are fully functional today.

Super AdminCan I edit a Company name after saving it?

No. Company names cannot be edited through the UI after creation. A typo is permanent unless fixed at the database level. Always confirm the exact legal entity name and spelling before clicking Create — the name will appear in every seller's SKU upload file.

Super AdminCan I remove a brand from a Company?

No. Brands cannot be removed once added. You can always add new brands to a Company, but you cannot delete existing ones. Plan and confirm the complete brand list with the seller before creating the Company to avoid polluting the dropdown with placeholder names.

Super AdminWhat's the difference between "All Brands" and "Specific Brands" when mapping?

"All Brands" gives the seller every current and future brand of that Company automatically — new brands added later flow in without any action needed. "Specific Brands" requires manual selection, and new brands added to the Company later will not automatically appear for that seller. Choose deliberately based on the distributor's actual portfolio.

Super AdminIs Serviceability mandatory before a seller can receive orders?

Yes, absolutely. Without a Beat Name and Delivery Day configured for each Company the seller carries, orders cannot route to that seller at all. The GeoJSON polygon can be added later, but the beat name and delivery day fields are required at setup.

GeneralWhat is ONDC and why does it matter?

ONDC (Open Network for Digital Commerce) is the open protocol that connects the Seller Store to buyer apps. For a product to appear to buyers and receive orders, it must be ONDC-compliant — meaning it needs a product image, valid units (Gram/Kg/Litre/ml), an active Serviceability setup, and the seller's ONDC connector configured.

My SKU rows are failing with "Manufacturer not found." What's wrong?

What units are accepted by ONDC?

Only: Gram, Kilogram, Litre, Millilitre. Use Gram as a workaround for Pieces/Pack/Nos.

I imported SKUs but buyers can't see them. Why?

I'm not receiving any orders.

Serviceability is not configured. Contact your Super Admin to set up Beat Name and Delivery Day for each Company you carry.

Can I change my warehouse address?

The dashboard says "Dashboard is on the way." Is something broken?

No — this is expected in Phase 1. All five sidebar modules are fully functional.

Can I edit a Company name after saving it?

No. Confirm the exact legal name before clicking Create. Typos require a database fix.

Can I remove a brand from a Company?

No. Brands can only be added, never removed. Plan the full list before saving.

All Brands vs Specific Brands — when do I use which?

Use All Brands for full-line distributors (future brands auto-include). Use Specific Brands for partial-portfolio distributors (manual updates required for new brands).

Is Serviceability mandatory before a seller can receive orders?

Yes. Beat Name + Delivery Day per Company is mandatory. Polygon can come later.

What happens if I enter wrong Lat/Long for a seller?

The coordinates are locked and become the warehouse. Incorrect coordinates break serviceability beat distances and routing. Escalate for a database fix — the seller cannot change it themselves.
