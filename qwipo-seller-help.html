FAQs

Common seller questions.

Quick answers for the most common issues distributors run into.

My SKU rows are failing with "Manufacturer not found." What's wrong?

The Manufacturer field must exactly match the Company name your Super Admin saved — character-by-character, including dots, spaces, and capitalisation. There is no fuzzy matching. Ask your Super Admin for the exact name and copy-paste it directly into the file.

What units are accepted by ONDC?

Only four units are accepted: Gram, Kilogram, Litre, and Millilitre. If your products use Pieces, Pack, or Nos — use Gram as a temporary workaround. This ONDC limitation is under review.

I successfully imported SKUs but buyers still can't see them. Why?

After import, all SKUs show "Non-compliant" because product images are missing. ONDC requires at least one image per SKU before it appears in the buyer app. Run the sku-image-upload process — status will flip to Compliant once images are uploaded.

I'm not receiving any orders. What should I check?

Confirm that your Super Admin has configured Serviceability for your account — specifically a Beat Name and Delivery Day for each Company you carry. Without this, orders cannot route to you even if your SKUs are live and compliant.

Can I change my warehouse address?

No. Your warehouse address is set during Seller profile creation and is permanently locked. If the coordinates are wrong, contact your Super Admin — it requires a database-level correction that cannot be done from the portal.

The file uploads fine but nothing actually imports. What happened?

You're using an old or self-made template. Always download a fresh template from My SKU → Bulk Import → Download Template before each upload. Column order and validation rules change between releases — reusing old files causes silent failures.

The dashboard says "Dashboard is on the way." Is something broken?

No — this is expected in Phase 1. Dashboard analytics are coming in a future release. All five sidebar modules — My SKU, Customers, Offers & Schemes, Orders, and Settings — are fully functional today.

What is ONDC and why does it matter for my catalogue?

ONDC (Open Network for Digital Commerce) is the open protocol connecting your store to buyer apps. For a product to be visible to buyers, it must be ONDC-compliant — meaning it needs a product image, valid units (Gram/Kg/Litre/ml), and your store must have Serviceability configured. Non-compliant SKUs are invisible to buyers.
